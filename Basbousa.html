Arabische Basbousa

Prep: 30 mins
Cook: 90 mins
Yields: 8 Servings
For Begginers:

Ingredients



01.

In a deep bowl, add farina, sugar, baking powder, eggs and everything else. Mixing everything well.

02.

Mix eveything together until it looks like that:

03.

Grease a tray with oil

04.

press the mix down onto square baking dish or round pie pan.The cake mix should be about 1 inch thick.Cut a diamond or square design in the cake with a butter knife.

05.

Place an almond or any other type of nut you have on hand onto each pre-cut square.

06.

Bake at 400 degrees for 30 min to 40 mins until it's a bronze brown color. If no color forms on the top turn on broiler for 1-2 minutes until the top is golden/bronze. Cut the Cake again along the pre-cut lines and pour cold syrup on top while its hot so it can absorb all through.

07.

prepare the serup: Put on water with sugar and boil for 10-20 minutes

08.

place the serup on the basbousa

09.

your basbousa is done: have fun eating!
